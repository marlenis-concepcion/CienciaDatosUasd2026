# UASD Virtual INF-8237 Assignment Requirements

Use these rules as hard constraints for assignments copied from UASD Virtual for INF-8237 Ciencia de Datos I.

## Delivery Format

- Document must be 100% APA format, adapted to the teacher's stated structure.
- Must include three types of citations:
  - Narrative citation.
  - Parenthetical citation.
  - Direct textual citation.
- Structure of document:
  1. Presentación
  2. Resumen
  3. Abstract
  4. Tabla de contenido
  5. Cuerpo/desarrollo
  6. Referencias
  7. Anexo
- Content/body length: maximum 5-10 pages. Do not count title page/presentation, resumen, table of contents, references, or annexes.

## Body Structure

- H1: the main topic/title of the paper.
- H2 sections:
  - Introducción
  - Marco de referencia
  - 3 to 5 topic subtitles required by the assignment
  - Conclusiones
- H3: use sparingly only when a subsection truly needs another level.

## Essay 1: Origen y Evolución de la Ciencia de Datos

Research the history and evolution of DSC using at least five high-impact peer-reviewed articles where authorities in the field address:

- Padre fundador.
- Primeras revistas o conferencias científicas.
- Minería de datos versus matemáticas estadísticas.
- Surgimiento de los algoritmos.
- Sociedades that lead the field.

Also include:

- A summarized timeline or infographic in the body of the DOCX as a figure.
- An annex with a QR code to access the timeline or infographic online.

## Formatting Preference for This Teacher

- The user prefers the formatted version with UASD logo and presentation style when allowed.
- If the teacher mentions Roman numerals, use lower Roman numerals for preliminary pages and restart Arabic numbering at 1 in the body.
- Keep APA substance strict even when using a more visual UASD presentation:
  - Correct citations and references.
  - Correct heading hierarchy.
  - Figure/table labels and notes.
  - Annex labels and titles.
  - References alphabetized and matched to citations.
